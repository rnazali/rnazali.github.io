title: Sunrise

Someday, when I am only a quiet memory carried by the wind of your years, 
I hope the sunrise will remind you of this moment—your hand in mine, 
a small flower trembling in the morning light—and you will know that once, 
at the dawn of the world, 
a father stood beside his daughter and wished he could hold that moment forever. 🌅🌼🤍
